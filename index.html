Classroom Monopoly: Auction-Only Edition

Designed for 21 Students / 7 Teams

Quick-Start Guide

  1. Divide into 7 teams of 3 players.
  2. Each team starts with $1,500 Monopoly money.
  3. Use 1 Monopoly board. Skip buying—go straight to auction when landing on a property.
  4. All teams can bid. Bidding starts at $1, increases in $10+ increments.
  5. Rotate team turns to roll dice and land on properties.
  6. Track team cash and property using the auction sheets.
  7. End game after time is up. Highest net worth wins!

Tips

  • Use a timer for turns (1 minute max per team).
  • Banker/Auctioneer runs the show—keeps things moving fast.
  • Optionally use a Google Sheet to track total cash/property values.
